About me

I design for the
humans behind
the screen.

I'm Autumn Grayson, a UX and product designer with experience at Meta and Oracle. I specialize in information architecture, design systems, and research-led product thinking — especially in complex, ambiguous problem spaces where the right question matters more than the first solution.

My work sits at the intersection of clarity and usability: reducing cognitive load, improving navigability, and making sure what gets built actually reflects what users need. I'm equally comfortable running user interviews on day one and iterating in Figma on day twenty.

Based in Seattle. Open to full-time UX/product design or management roles — particularly in tech, healthcare, and consumer-facing products.
